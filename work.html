My work / My future work

Business Analyst

A business analyst analyzes an organization's business processes and systems, identifies areas for improvement, and develops and implements solutions to increase efficiency and productivity. They collaborate closely with stakeholders to comprehend their needs and translate them into actionable plans and specifications for IT teams and other stakeholders. Business analysts also conduct research and data analysis in order to identify business trends and make recommendations for strategies that are in line with organizational objectives.

Data Analyst

A data analyst is in charge of gathering, processing, and analyzing large datasets to identify patterns, trends, and insights that can be used to inform business decisions. Data analysts frequently collaborate with business stakeholders to identify key metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs), as well as to create reports and visualizations that effectively communicate their findings. In addition, they use statistical modeling and predictive analytics to generate forecasts and insights that can be used to inform strategic planning and decision-making.

Healthcare / Life Science Consultant

A healthcare/life science consultant is responsible for providing strategic and operational guidance to healthcare and life science organizations such as pharmaceutical companies, medical device manufacturers, hospitals, and healthcare providers. They collaborate with clients to identify areas for improvement, develop solutions, and implement best practices to improve operational efficiency, reduce costs, and improve patient outcomes. They may conduct research and analysis to identify market trends and opportunities, as well as assist clients in developing and launching new products and services.
